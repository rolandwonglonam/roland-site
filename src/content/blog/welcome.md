Welcome to my corner of the internet.

This site serves three purposes. First, it's a home for my academic work — my PhD research on low-sodium salt substitutes and cardiovascular disease prevention at the University of Queensland. Second, it's the public face of Wayne Insight Spring, where I help families navigate the complex world of international medical education. Third, it's a space for sharing ideas about research thinking, decision-making, and how AI tools are reshaping how we work.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about the intersection of health science, education, and technology. Some posts will be technical; others will be reflective. All of them will aim to be useful.

If any of this resonates, I'd love to hear from you. Head over to the [contact page](/contact) and say hello.
